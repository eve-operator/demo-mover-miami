## Outreach Strategy
1. **SMS/Text** via Google Voice (preferred for local businesses)
2. **Email** as backup
3. **Call** for high-value leads

## Message Templates
### Option A (Short & Direct)
```
Hi [Name], I noticed [Business] doesn't have a website yet. I help Miami small businesses get found online. Quick call to see if it makes sense? - [Your Name]
```

### Option B (Value-First)
```
Hi [Name], I'm reaching out because I saw [Business] has great reviews but no website. Many of your competitors are getting customers online - want me to show you how? No pressure. - [Your Name]
```

### Option C (Modern/ casual)
```
Hey [Business] 👋 No website? You're missing out on customers searching for you on Google right now. I build affordable sites for Miami businesses - happy to show you what you're missing. 
```
