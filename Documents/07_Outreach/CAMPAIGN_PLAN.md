## Outreach Strategy

### Multi-Channel Approach
| Channel | Volume | Response Rate | Best For |
|---------|--------|---------------|----------|
| SMS/Text | 50/day | 9.3% | Local businesses |
| Email | 30/day | 2-5% | With emails found |
| Cold Call | 10/day | 3-8% | High-value leads |

### Message Templates (tested variants)

**SMS Option A (short)**
```
Hi [Name], noticed [Business] has great reviews but no website. I buildaffordable sites for Miami businesses - want to see a free demo? [Name]
```

**SMS Option B (curiosity)**
```
Does [Business] show up when people Google "[service] Miami"? Free audit - can show you what's missing.
```

**Email (formal)**
```
Subject: Free website audit for [Business]

Hi [Name],

[Business] has [X] great reviews but no website. You're missing customers who search for your services online.

I've built sites for [X] Miami businesses. Happy to show you a free demo - no obligation.

Book 15 min here: [calendar link]

Best,
[Your Name]
```
